# INS Product Positioning v1

## Product name

**INS — Instancia Neural Soberana**

English descriptor:

**Sovereign Neural Instance**

## Official definition

INS es una Instancia Neural Soberana: una instancia de IA local-first con identidad y memoria persistentes en el dispositivo, motores de razonamiento elegidos por el usuario y capacidad de crecimiento progresivo.

## English definition

INS is a Sovereign Neural Instance: a local-first AI instance with persistent on-device identity and memory, user-selected reasoning engines, and progressive growth.

## Product distinction

INS is the persistent local instance.

Reasoning providers are replaceable computation engines. They are not the identity, memory authority or executor.

## Architecture pillars

1. Identity
2. Memory
3. Reasoning
4. Growth

## Claims allowed

- Local-first
- Persistent on-device identity and memory
- User-selected reasoning engines
- Governed progressive growth
- External reasoning is optional and replaceable

## Claims not allowed yet

- Fully local AI
- No data ever leaves the device
- Fully autonomous
- Automatic self-modification
- Production-ready
