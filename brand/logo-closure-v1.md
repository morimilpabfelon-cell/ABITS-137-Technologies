# ABITS-137 Logo Closure v1

## Approved assets

- `a137-mark.svg`: primary detailed mark for interfaces and presentation covers.
- `a137-mark-compact.svg`: simplified mark for favicon and small UI sizes.
- `a137-mark-mono-dark.svg`: monochrome mark for light surfaces.
- `a137-mark-mono-light.svg`: monochrome mark for dark surfaces.
- `icons/icon-180.png`: Apple touch icon.
- `icons/icon-192.png`: web app icon.
- `icons/icon-512.png`: high-resolution web app icon.

## Minimum sizes

- Detailed mark: 32 px minimum.
- Compact mark: 16 px minimum.
- Institutional lockup: 220 px minimum width.

## Clear space

Keep clear space around the mark equal to at least one quarter of its width.

## Restrictions

- Do not add extra glow.
- Do not recolor individual elements.
- Do not stretch or rotate the mark.
- Do not place it on visually noisy backgrounds.
- Do not use the detailed mark as a 16 px favicon.
