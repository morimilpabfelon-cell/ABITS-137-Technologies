# Brand Strategy

## Brand Name

**ABITS-137 Technologies**

The name should be treated as a serious technology company name, not as a casual product label. Public usage should maintain consistent capitalization and spacing.

Preferred lockup:

```txt
ABITS-137
Technologies
```

## Positioning

**Operational Intelligence Infrastructure**

ABITS-137 builds systems for AI-powered operations: agents, runtime control, auditability, security, and workflow execution.

## What ABITS-137 Is

ABITS-137 is a technology company focused on:

- enterprise AI agent systems;
- secure automation;
- operational runtime infrastructure;
- audit and governance layers;
- intelligent workflow execution.

## What ABITS-137 Is Not

ABITS-137 should not be positioned as:

- a generic chatbot company;
- a hype-only AI startup;
- a consumer entertainment app;
- a vague “future of AI” brand;
- an ASI company without proof.

## Tone

The tone should be institutional, technical, precise, and ambitious.

Use language like:

- runtime;
- governance;
- audit layer;
- secure execution;
- operational intelligence;
- agent systems;
- enterprise workflows;
- control architecture.

Avoid language like:

- revolutionary AI magic;
- chatbot for everything;
- the future is here;
- ASI for everyone;
- unlimited intelligence.
